About This Project

The Islamic Resource Hub is a personal project by Iederees Francis, gathering dhikr, qasidah, nasheed and related Islamic media into one easy place, especially for the Cape Town community and beyond.

Wherever possible, recordings are sourced from trusted local circles and made available for easy listening and downloading, so that remembrance of Allah ﷻ can be part of everyday life.
